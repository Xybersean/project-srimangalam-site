## 📍 Restaurant Intelligence (Research Phase 1)

**Name**: Sri Mangalam A/C Soshigaya-Okura (スリマンガラム 祖師ヶ谷大蔵店)
**Location**: Tokyo, Setagaya City, Soshigaya, 3-33-2 Sanko Building B1F (2 mins from Soshigaya-Okura Station).

### 🏆 Reputation & Ratings

- **Tabelog**: Award-winning status (**Hyakumeiten 2024**). High trust and popularity among Tokyo's foodies.
- **Google Maps**: Strong local presence with high ratings.
- **History**: Opened in Soshigaya Okura on May 6, 2022, after a successful stint in Kyodo.
- **Tabelog Avg. Price**:
  - Lunch: ¥1,000–¥1,999
  - Dinner: ¥2,000–¥2,999

### 🍱 Cuisine & Aesthetic

- **Specialization**: Authentic South Indian (specifically **Chettinad/Tamil** cuisine).
- **Owner/Chef**: **Mahalingam (Maha-san)**, a master of South Indian spices who brought the authentic banana leaf meal style to Japan.
- **Key Feature**: Signature dishes served on **banana leaves**, creating a vibrant and authentic visual experience.
- **Signature Performance**: Traditional "Long-pour Chai" (Teh Tarik style) often performed at the table.
- **Vibe**: Aromatic, spice-forward, and communal. The design of the website should reflect this richness and warmth.

### 🌐 Digital Presence

- **Instagram**: [@srimangalam_soshigaya](https://www.instagram.com/srimangalam_soshigaya/) (Primary source for visual assets).
- **YouTube**: Mentions of a channel exist; awaiting sync with **Ryo** for the specific URL and asset list.
- **Current Barrier**: Excellent culinary product and high social engagement, but lacks a centralized, multi-functional website for menus, history, and reservations.
